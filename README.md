## Book-stock store :books:
#### _Book-stock is a large bookstore with about 80000 titles of books. Much attention in the assortment is paid to childrens literature and educational games for children. The pride of the store is the department of gift and exclusive editions - unique books released in limited editions. The range also includes related products, including puzzles, postcards, bookmarks, cartographic publications, souvenirs._
## Our values :two_hearts:
#### _We are driven by the desire to meet the needs of customers, the professionalism and involvement of our employees. The focus on meeting the needs of our customers is the fundamental principle of the organization of our work._
## Buyer :moneybag:
#### _We are focused on an individual approach to everyone. The buyer's trust is more important to us than profit. We are focused on stable and long-term cooperation .And we want every client to find everything that interests him._
## Professionalism :relaxed:
#### _We are aware that success lies in the competence of our employees, and we do everything possible to attract and retain the best specialists, creating conditions for the development of their professional qualities. ## Team spirit :star:
